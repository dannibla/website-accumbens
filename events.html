All undergraduate students are qualified including CRRI's but it is restricted to one CRRI per team.

  • First stage will be the prelims followed by the second round of onstage quiz consisting of six teams.

Topic :

On the topic of your choice

Rules :
  • All abstracts must be submitted to “accumbens2k19@gmail.com” with subject line “Case presentation {name of participant}”
  • Last date for submission of abstract 25 th May 2019.
Abstract format :
  • All abstracts must be presented as “.doc/.docx” format only.

  • Abstract must be submitted along with completed registration form with details of full payment.
