About Us

Accumbens, medquiz and workshop with the introduction of new subjects and innovations presented by Meenakshi Medical College

Throughout the years, Meenakshi Medical College has escalated in conducting exemplary events that highlight their insight towards education in par excellence.

Each year these impassioned events are being conducted by the pre-final mbbs students.

“Accumbens” - medquiz and workshop was first conducted in the year 2014 and has hence been an awe-inspiring tradition.

With the introduction of new subjects in our workshop and many new additional innovations we wish to be prodigious like those before us.
